About Us

We help teenagers take their first step towards a professional world.

STEMiner had started with the vision to empower the younger generation and help them build successful careers. The idea might have been inspired by the growing interest in technology, entrepreneurship, and leadership among the youth.

The founder of STEMiner which is Prisha Basak recognized the need for a platform that could connect teenagers with mentors, peers, and opportunities to help them realize their potential and achieve their career goals. By creating a community network platform, We aims to provide a supportive environment where young people can connect, learn, grow and succeed.

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive space for teenagers to connect with each other, access resources and mentorship, and develop their skills and interests.

Through its platform, We aims to empower teenagers to build their personal and professional networks, explore new opportunities, and unleash their full potential.

Our vision is to empower and inspire the next generation of leaders by creating a trusted, mentorship-driven community where teenagers can connect, learn, and grow.

The platform aims to provide a safe and supportive environment where young people can explore their passions, build valuable skills, and make meaningful connections with mentors, peers, and businesses. By fostering a community of growth-minded individuals, TeenVisor aims to help shape the future and equip the next generation with the tools they need to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

STEMiner Entrepreneurship Camp

Welcome to the STEMiner Entrepreneurship Camp for Young Students!
Our goal is to inspire and educate the next generation of entrepreneurs and provide them with the skills and knowledge they need to start and run a successful business. Join us for a week-long adventure into the world of entrepreneurship.
